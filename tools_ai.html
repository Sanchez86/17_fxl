Forex financial market is based on the currency exchange and is the most popular among traders worldwide with a daily turnover of $5.3 trillion.

Trades on Forex are profitable while you buy one currency for the low price and sell it for high, exchanging it for another one. The price is based on the exchange rate and the revenue or loss is based on its difference. It is almost the same as when you convert money at the post office. Traders often take the advantage of global financial and geopolitical events: in such a period exchange rate is extremely volatile.

Invented in 1981, stock indices display the overall condition of all the publicly traded companies within the particular group. Such index as American NASDAQ includes stocks from 3000 companies, the most well-known among which are Apple, Google, Microsoft, Oracle, Amazon and Intel.

Changes in each stock value affect the indices on the whole. An index trading is a unique form of stock exchange activity: a trader should use strategies and techniques which are different from those common on Forex or commodities market.
