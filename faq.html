What is a CFD?

A CFD (a Contract for Difference) is an agreement between a buyer and a seller to exchange the difference between the opening price and closing price of a financial instrument. This instrument allows you to trade the change between the prices of the asset underlying it.

What instruments are offered?

What is leverage?

Leverage is borrowed capital that allows traders make larger investments and get higher profits. Note that it is associated with the higher risk and potential losses. For example, a trader using 100:1 leverage can make a $10,000 investment with $100.

What is Stop Loss/Take Profit?

A Stop Loss protects you in case the price reaches a certain level. We highly recommend to set a Stop Loss every time you open a new position. Take Profit closes the position when it reaches a preset profit point.
