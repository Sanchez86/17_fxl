CFD – Contract for Difference

Contracts for difference (CFDs) give a trader an opportunity to take the advantage of a great selection of the various assets without purchasing them. This tool is primarily intended for independent traders and investors who don't possess enough savings for acquiring such positions. CFDs have established itself as a reliable and transparent investment instrument and became popular in the international arena.

The main concept of CFD trading is that you are getting a benefit from price fluctuations by investing in the prices’ difference. It involves a significant risk to lose due to price reduction by the end of a contract but provides you with a chance to gain a lot in case of the rise of an asset's value.
